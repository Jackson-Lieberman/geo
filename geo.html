Project Overview

CleanCrop.AI is a machine learning model that intelligently detects deseases in crops.
Allowing farmers to discover deseases in their plants effectively and efficiently.

Features

  • Automatic detection of desease.
  • Provides insight on treatments.
  • Optimized for a variety of plant species.
  • Simple, user-friendly interface.
  • Uses Python & TensorFlow.

What I learned

  • This was my first time building an AI model from scratch
  • Got to do research on the effects crop desease has on world hunger
  • Learned how to use TensorFlow
  • Got to spend time researching data sets and diseases for plants
